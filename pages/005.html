It's more than just a game or a software 1.0 milestone.. I have to bring myself back to my original state often because I get carried away easily. I'm just an artist and my game of life is to make art, to grow and to learn along the way. Dali said if you want to make a masterpiece, don't sit down and try to make it... you have to just paint over and over until naturally it comes~ in other words, he says, if you want to make a masterpiece, it has to come to you... I have fallen victim to this mindset many times, growing fixated on achieving a 'masterpiece', but luckily it doesn't consume me to the point of no return...
